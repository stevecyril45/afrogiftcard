How to Sign Up?

Afro Gift Card is accepted only in verified partner stores. Our trusted partners form the exclusive network where every Afro Gift Card is valid. Interested businesses can register to become a partner, receive a unique API key, and access a dedicated partner dashboard to manage their store and track redemptions.

Signing up is fast and straightforward. Once registered, you’ll get instant access to our partner resources and technical support to help integrate Afro Gift Card seamlessly into your payment system. With zero setup hassle and real-time tracking tools, you can start benefiting from gift card redemptions right away.

Requirements to Become a Partner

Prepare these details before applying:

  • Business Name - Registered with CAC or equivalent authority.
  • Contact Information - Full name of the primary contact.
  • Registration Type - Sole Proprietorship, Limited Liability, Public Liability, or NGO.
  • Business Number (CAC) - Valid number (minimum 5 characters).
  • Agreement to Terms - Accept our two sets of terms and conditions.

How to Become a Partner

  1. Step 1: Authentication - Login using email.
  2. Step 2: Registration - Click "New Business" to register a new business.
  3. Step 3: Verification - Reviewed within 1-24 hours (CAC priority).
  4. Step 4: Start Accepting - Track redemptions via your dashboard.
