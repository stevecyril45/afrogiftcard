This Privacy Policy explains how Afro Gift Card (“we,” “our,” “us”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use our website, mobile applications, and services. 1. Introduction

Afro Gift Card is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal data. Our platform serves individuals and businesses who wish to buy, sell, or exchange gift cards securely. This Privacy Policy outlines in detail the types of information we collect, why we collect it, and how it is handled.

The protection of your data is not just a legal requirement but also a core part of our values as a financial-technology company. We operate in accordance with applicable privacy laws including, where relevant, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR), and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

2. Information We Collect

We collect different categories of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Identifiers: such as your full name, phone number, email address, and government-issued ID where applicable.
  • Account Information: login credentials, security questions, and profile details.
  • Transaction Information: records of gift card sales, purchases, payment methods, and transaction history.
  • Technical Information: device identifiers, IP address, browser type, operating system, and cookies.
  • Communications: chat history, emails, and support interactions with our team.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To verify your identity and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • To process transactions and deliver purchased gift cards.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • To send service-related updates, promotions, and newsletters.
  • To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.
