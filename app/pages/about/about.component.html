Who We Are

AfroGift is Africa’s leading digital marketplace for buying, selling, and redeeming gift cards with ease. We are not just a platform; we are a movement to bridge Africa with the global digital economy. Born out of the need to solve trust issues, unfair rates, and slow payments in the gift card trading industry, AfroGift was built to empower Africans with a secure, transparent, and rewarding ecosystem.

We make trading gift cards easy, fast, and rewarding. Our user-friendly platform ensures that you get the best value for your cards, while our team works behind the scenes to keep every transaction safe and seamless. Join thousands of happy users who trust us for their digital trading needs.

Our Story

We started AfroGift with one mission: to eliminate barriers in the digital exchange space for Africans. What began as a small innovation has grown into a pan-African ecosystem trusted by individuals, SMEs, and corporate organizations across multiple countries.

Our growth story is built on one thing: Trust. Today, AfroGift continues to expand with a vision to be the leading African-owned digital trading hub recognized globally.
