Our History

In 2019, after repeatedly facing the frustration of inconsistent rates and delayed payments while trading gift cards, a group of young entrepreneurs in Lagos saw the need for a better, more reliable solution. Determined to transform the digital trading experience, they began building a platform that prioritized speed, security, and simplicity for everyday users.

After months of testing, development, and customer feedback, Afro Gift Card officially launched, quickly gaining recognition as a trustworthy name in the gift card trading space across Nigeria. What started as a simple exchange platform has since grown into a robust service offering competitive rates, 24/7 customer support, and seamless user experience for both buyers and sellers.

Today, Afro Gift Card continues to evolve—expanding its reach, improving its technology, and empowering users with the freedom to trade digital assets safely and confidently, all from the palm of their hand.
